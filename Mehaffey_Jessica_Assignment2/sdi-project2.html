Once again, I went back to my job at a cupcake bakery for my inspiration.

Lately, we have been baking dozens upon dozens of cupcakes for both the stock floor and for orders. I decided to put my overworked hands to use once again and create a code that shows the sheer volume of baked goods we can create on any given day at the shop I work at.
