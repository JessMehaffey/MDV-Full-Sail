Every single day at the cupcake shop is an adventure. Whether I am wrist-deep in buttercream icing or up to my eyeballs in cake orders, I never have a moment to just sit down and relax.

Since I am co-manager as well as the head decorator, I have much more on my plate than most of the girls that work with me. If I'm not decorating, I'm filling out more paperwork than a normal human should have to fill out.

The last two assignments gave a sneak peek into how I go about training employees. This assignment revolves around the other aspect of my co-managing.
